What is Mental Health?

Mental health is our social, psychological, and emotional well-being. The importance of mental health cannot be understated. It essentially effects every part of our lives, especially given that mental illnesses can lead to physical ails as well.

You're not alone. Poor mental health and mental illnesses are common in the United States. Many mental illnesses can lead you to believe that nobody else could understand what you're experiencing or even cause you to believe that nobody cares. We care and we want to help you.
